# REITs — Emerging Markets Value Strategy

**Method:** P/NAV with Country-Specific Cap Rates + FFO Multiples

## Key Metrics & Adjustments

| Metric | DM Typical | EM Adjustment | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| **Cap Rates** | 3-9% range | 7-12% range | Higher risk premium |
| **P/FFO** | 12-18x | 10-14x | Lower multiples vs DM |
| **Debt/EBITDA** | <5.0x | <4.0x | Stricter leverage (less forgiving market) |
| **Dividend Yield** | 3-5% | 4-7% | Higher yields compensate for risk |
| **Currency Risk** | - | +2-3% adjustment to discount rate | FX conversion risk |

## China REITs

- Real estate market uncertainty post-2022 property crisis
- REITs mandated to have China property exposure (cannot diversify internationally)
- Cap rates elevated (8-10%+) reflecting risk
- Valuation discount 40%+ vs developed market REITs justified

## India REITs

- Limited REIT market; mostly office/industrial
- Urbanization tailwind; cap rates gradually compressing
- P/NAV 0.9-1.1x typical
- Dividend yield 5-6% attractive but carry higher risk than DM REITs
