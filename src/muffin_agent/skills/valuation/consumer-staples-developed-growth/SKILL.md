# Consumer Staples — Developed Markets Growth Strategy

**Method:** P/E + ROIC + Market Share Expansion

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Strategic Basis |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| **P/E Ratio** | 18-28x | Premium if growth >8% sustained |
| **ROIC** | >15% | Capital efficiency in growth phase |
| **Revenue Growth** | 5-8% YoY | From emerging market exposure, innovation |
| **Gross Margin** | Expanding | Mix shift to premium products |
| **Operating Leverage** | Visible | Margin expansion from revenue growth |
| **Share Buybacks** | Moderate | 1-2% annual reduction; conservative |

## Growth Drivers

- Emerging market exposure (higher growth rates 8-12% vs developed 2-3%)
- Innovation in existing categories (premium/organic segments)
- M&A of complementary brands (Nestlé, Unilever model)
