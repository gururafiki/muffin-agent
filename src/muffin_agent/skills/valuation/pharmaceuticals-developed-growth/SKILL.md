# Pharmaceuticals — Developed Markets Growth Strategy

**Method:** rNPV of Pipeline + New Product Launches + Market Expansion

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Strategic Implication |
|--------|--------|----------------------|
| **P/E Ratio** | 15-25x | Premium if strong Phase 3 data |
| **Blockbuster Pipeline** | 2-3 drugs Phase 3 | $500M+ peak sales forecast |
| **Clinical Trial Success** | Phase 3 efficacy data | Market impact, peak sales confirmation |
| **Patent Cliff Timeline** | No material cliff next 5 years | Or strong pipeline replacement |
| **Gross Margin Trend** | Stable/expanding | Cost of goods control important |

## New Drug Peak Sales Forecasting

- Market size × expected penetration rate
- Example: $50B pain market × 10% share = $5B potential
- Discount for: side effects, competitive entrants, pricing pressure
- Realistic peak: $2-3B for successful differentiated drug
