# Software/SaaS — Emerging Markets Value Strategy

**Method:** PEG <1.0 + Significant Revenue Multiple Discount

## Key Metrics & Adjustments

| Metric | DM Range | EM Target | Rationale |
|--------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| **EV/Revenue** | 5-8x | 4-6x | 30-40% discount typical |
| **PEG Ratio** | <1.0 | <0.8 | More conservative threshold |
| **NRR Target** | >100% | >90% minimum | Building category, churn higher |
| **Gross Margin** | >60% | >50% acceptable | Lower efficiency early stage |
| **Currency Risk Adjustment** | - | -10-15% | FX volatility adjustment |

## Why Lower Multiples

- Market maturity: payment infrastructure, credit card penetration less developed
- Customer creditworthiness: higher credit risk, payment delays
- Currency depreciation risk: ARR in local currency subject to conversion risk
- Competitive dynamics: often more competitors, pricing pressure

## India SaaS

- Strong enterprise SaaS growth, but customer acquisition costs 30-40% higher vs US
- Adjust EV/Revenue from 8x (US growth SaaS) to 5-6x for India equivalent
