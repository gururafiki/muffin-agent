# Consumer Discretionary — Emerging Markets Value Strategy

**Method:** P/S Multiple with EM Discount + Price-Sensitive Dynamics

## Key Metrics & Adjustments

| Metric | DM Target | EM Target | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **P/S Ratio** | 1.0-2.0x | 0.6-1.3x | 40% discount |
| **EV/EBITDA** | 8-12x | 6-10x | Lower multiples |
| **Operating Margin** | 8-12% | 6-10% | Earlier stage operations |
| **Debt/EBITDA** | <3.0x | <2.5x | Less forgiving credit |
| **Currency Risk** | - | -10-15% adjustment | FX conversion risk |

## Price Sensitivity

- Consumer discretionary in emerging markets highly price-sensitive
- Income elasticity high (2-3x); rising wages drive disproportionate demand
- But macro downturns hit hard (consumption first to be cut)
