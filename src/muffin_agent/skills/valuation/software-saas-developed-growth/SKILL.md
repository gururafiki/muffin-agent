# Software/SaaS — Developed Markets Growth Strategy

**Method:** PEG 1.0-2.5 + Revenue Growth Acceleration + Customer Expansion Metrics

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Validation |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **Revenue Growth** | 25%+ YoY | Minimum for premium valuation |
| **PEG Ratio** | 1.5-2.0 | Acceptable if NRR >120% and strong unit economics |
| **EV/Revenue** | 10-15x | Premium multiples justified by growth |
| **NRR** | >120% | Land-and-expand model working |
| **Gross Retention Rate** | >90% | Sticky product; low churn |
| **Rule of 40 Target** | 30% growth + 10% FCF margin | Balanced profitability |
| **Magic Number** | >0.75 | (Current quarter ARR growth rate / prior quarter S&M spend) indicates CAC efficiency |
| **CAC Payback Period** | <18 months | Customer acquisition cost paid back quickly |

## Growth Validation

- Ensure revenue growth not slowing (watch quarterly trend)
- NRR stability check: Is expansion revenue decelerating?
- Unit economics improving or worsening (CAC payback critical)
