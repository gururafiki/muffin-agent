# Telecommunications — Emerging Markets Growth Strategy

**Method:** Subscriber Growth + ARPU Expansion + Data Revenue Acceleration

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Validation |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **Subscriber Growth** | 8-15% YoY | Market penetration in rural areas |
| **ARPU Growth** | 5-10% YoY | 3G→4G→5G migration |
| **Data Revenue Growth** | 15-25% | Fastest growing segment |
| **Market Share Gains** | Visible | Subscriber gains outpacing competition |
| **Churn Improvement** | -100-300 bps | Loyalty programs, bundling success |

## India Example (2024-2025)

- Jio (Reliance): Growing at 3-4% in mature 1.3B subscriber market
- ARPU expansion modest (price competition intense)
- Data consumption growing 30%+ YoY (huge upside if monetized)
- 5G rollout: potential $3-5/month ARPU uplift by 2027-2028
