# Consumer Discretionary — Developed Markets Growth Strategy

**Method:** P/E + Sales Growth + Margin Expansion

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Validation |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **Revenue Growth** | 8-15% YoY | Market share gains + category growth |
| **P/E Ratio** | 15-25x | Higher for proven growth |
| **Gross Margin** | Expanding | Mix improvement, pricing |
| **EBITDA Margin** | +200-300 bps expansion | Operating leverage visible |
| **Debt/EBITDA** | <2.5x | Financial flexibility maintained |
| **Same-Store Sales (Retail)** | Positive 3%+ | Comparable location sales growth |
| **Customer Traffic** | Growing | Volume gains, not just pricing |
