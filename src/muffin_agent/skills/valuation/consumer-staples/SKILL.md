# Consumer Staples Valuation — Common Principles

## Why Lower P/E

- Predictable but low growth
- Defensive characteristics appeal to income investors
- Quality of earnings high but limited expansion upside
- Better suited for valuation multiples than growth multiples

## Brand Moat Analysis

- Can company sustain pricing above inflation?
- Market share stability over 5-year periods?
- New product/category success rate?
- Geographic/demographic trends (demographic headwinds in developed markets)

## Pricing Power Assessment

- Historical price increases accepted by consumers?
- Volume impact of price increases (elasticity)?
- Competitive positioning: can competitors undercut?
- Input cost pass-through ability
