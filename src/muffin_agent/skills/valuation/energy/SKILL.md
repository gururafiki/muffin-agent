# Energy/Oil & Gas Valuation — Common Principles

## Why Not P/E

P/E is meaningless for oil & gas — earnings are too volatile with commodity cycles. Use FCF yield instead.

## Total Average Cost (TAC) Analysis

```
TAC = (Cost to Acquire + Cost to Build + Cost to Operate) / Total Barrels Produced
```

| Cost Tier | $/barrel | Examples |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Low-cost** | <$40/bbl | Middle East, Russia |
| **Mid-cost** | $40-60/bbl | North Sea, some US shale |
| **High-cost** | $60-80/bbl | Canadian oil sands |

Compare TAC to current WTI benchmark for sustainability assessment.

## Reserve Life Analysis

| Reserve Life | Assessment |
|-------------|------------|
| <5 years | High replacement risk, depletion threat |
| 5-10 years | Need visible replacement strategy |
| >10 years | Comfortable runway |
