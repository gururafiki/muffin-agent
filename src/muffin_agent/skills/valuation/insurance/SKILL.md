# Insurance Valuation — Common Principles

## Subsector Distinction

Insurance valuation differs fundamentally by subsector:
- **General Insurance:** P/B + Combined Ratio + Investment Income
- **Life Insurance:** Embedded Value (EV) Multiple + New Business Value

## Core Insurance Quality Indicators

| Metric | General Insurance | Life Insurance |
|--------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Primary multiple** | P/B 0.7-1.5x | P/EV 0.6-1.0x |
| **Profitability** | Combined Ratio <100% | New Business Margin |
| **Income quality** | Underwriting + Investment | PVFP + ANAV |
| **Distribution** | Expense ratio <30% | Embedded Value growth |
