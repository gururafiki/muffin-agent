# Industrials — Developed Markets Growth Strategy

**Method:** EV/EBITDA + Organic & Inorganic Growth + Margin Expansion

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Driver |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **EV/EBITDA** | 10-15x | Premium for quality + growth |
| **Revenue Growth** | 8-12% YoY | Organic + acquisition contribution |
| **Order Backlog Growth** | 15%+ | Market share gains |
| **EBITDA Margin** | Expanding 200+ bps | Operating leverage |
| **ROIC** | >15% | Superior capital deployment |
| **Capex** | 4-5% of revenue | Capacity for growth without excess investment |

## Operational Efficiency Drivers

- Automation reducing manual labor costs
- Lean manufacturing adopted
- Supply chain optimization
- Digital transformation (Industry 4.0)
