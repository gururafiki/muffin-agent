# Pharmaceuticals — Emerging Markets Growth Strategy

**Method:** Revenue Growth in Underpenetrated Markets + Margin Expansion

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Validation |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **Domestic Revenue Growth** | 12-18% YoY | Category expansion, market share |
| **Export Growth** | 15%+ YoY | Regulatory approvals in new markets |
| **Margin Expansion** | +200-300 bps | Manufacturing scale, API cost leverage |
| **R&D Capability Building** | Measurable | Number of new drug approvals, pipeline |
| **Biosimilar Portfolio** | Growing market share | Path to $5-10B+ revenue potential |
