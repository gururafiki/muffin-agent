# Growth Stock Valuation Principles

## Core Growth Metrics (All Sectors)

- **Operating margin is NOT the primary metric** for growth stocks
- Focus instead on revenue growth, market share, and customer metrics
- Profitability threshold: break-even to positive FCF acceptable if growth justifies it
- **PEG Ratio < 2.0** generally indicates attractive entry for growth stocks
- Unit economics matter: CAC payback, LTV/CAC ratio
- Revenue quality: recurring vs one-time, expansion vs new customer
