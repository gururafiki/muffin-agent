# Industrials — Emerging Markets Common Adjustments

## Poland Manufacturing Advantage

- Labor cost 60-70% discount vs Western Europe
- EU member: regulatory certainty, market access
- Skilled workforce: automotive, electronics clusters
- Valuation: 10-20% premium to non-EU CEE (lower risk)

## China Manufacturing

- Cost advantage eroding (wages rising 5%+ annually)
- Geopolitical risk: supply chain fragmentation
- Overcapacity in certain sectors (EVs, batteries, steel)
- Valuation discount 30-40% reflects political risks
