# Consumer Discretionary Valuation — Common Principles

## Why P/S Over P/E

- Earnings highly cyclical; P/E can be misleading
- During downturns, earnings approach zero or go negative
- P/S more stable metric; reflects business scale
- EV/EBITDA provides capital structure adjustment

## Subsector Variations

| Subsector | Typical Margins | Notes |
|-----------|----------------|-------|
| **Retail (hardlines)** | 15-25% | Higher ROIC pressure |
| **Restaurants** | 8-12% | Franchise model (asset-light) |
| **Automotive** | 3-5% | Cyclical, high capex |
| **Luxury goods** | 40-50% | Premium pricing power |

## Cyclical Entry Strategy

- Buy when consumer confidence at trough (value trap risk critical)
- Look for: strong balance sheets, multiple cash generation, reasonable debt
- Avoid: overleveraged businesses exposed to recession
