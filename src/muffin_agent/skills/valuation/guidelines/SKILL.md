# Valuation Guidelines

## Quick Reference by Sector & Stock Type

| Sector | Value Primary Metric | Growth Primary Metric | DM P/Multiple Range | EM Discount | Key Risk |
|--------|----------------------|----------------------|-------------------|------------|----------|
| **Banking** | P/B + ROE | ROE expansion | 0.8-2.0x P/B | 30-40% | Asset quality, rates |
| **Insurance** | P/B + Ratios | Combined Ratio improve | 0.7-1.5x P/B | 30-40% | Catastrophe risk |
| **SaaS** | PEG <1.0 + NRR | Growth >25% + NRR 120% | 5-8x EV/Rev | 30-40% | Unit economics |
| **Pharma** | P/E + pipeline | rNPV growth | 10-15x | 30-40% | Patent cliff |
| **REITs** | P/NAV + FFO | NOI growth | 12-18x P/FFO | 30-40% | Cap rates |
| **Consumer Staples** | P/E + moat | Growth >8% + ROIC | 15-22x | 20-30% | Volume growth |

| **Industrials** | EV/EBITDA + FCF | Margin expansion | 6-10x | 30% | Capex risk |
| **Energy** | FCF yield | Production growth | 6-10% yield | 30-40% | Oil price risk |
| **Telecom** | EV/EBITDA + ARPU | ARPU + 5G growth | 6-8x | 30-40% | Competition |

## Verification Discipline

- All multiples should be checked against 2-3 year historical range
- Peer comparison: identify truly comparable companies
- Analyst consensus: check if your valuation deviates >30% from consensus
- Sensitivities: model what happens if key assumption changes by 1%

## Valuation Errors to Avoid

- **Banking:** Don't use P/E alone — balance sheet matters (use P/B cross-check)
- **SaaS:** Don't ignore unit economics — impressive NRR with high CAC = burn
- **Pharma:** Don't ignore patent cliffs — single-drug concentration risk
- **REITs:** Don't ignore cap rate environment — falling rates = rising valuations
- **Energy:** Don't use P/E — earnings too volatile; use FCF yield

## Discount Rates in Emerging Markets

- Do NOT simply add 3-5% country risk premium (McKinsey research)
- Instead: stress test specific business risks in scenario analysis
- Triangulate: DCF + comparable multiples + scenario analysis

## Currency Risk

- INR depreciation ~5% annually typical
- BRL, ZAR more volatile (10-15% moves common)
- Adjust valuations 10-15% for FX risk or require currency hedge
