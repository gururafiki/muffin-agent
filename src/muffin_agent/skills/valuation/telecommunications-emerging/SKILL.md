# Telecommunications — Emerging Markets Common Adjustments

## ARPU by Region (2024-2025)

| Region | ARPU (monthly) | Characteristics |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| **India** | $5-7 | Highest competition; 4-5 operators |
| **China** | $8-12 | Controlled market |
| **MENA** | ~$13 | Low by global standards |
| **Thailand** | $10-15 | Developed EM market |
| **Latin America** | $6-8 | Mixed maturity |

## Competitive Dynamics

- India: most intense (4-5 major operators competing fiercely)
- High churn typical (40%+ annual in India)
- Pricing pressure: consumers highly price-sensitive
- ARPU stagnation/decline common in competitive markets
