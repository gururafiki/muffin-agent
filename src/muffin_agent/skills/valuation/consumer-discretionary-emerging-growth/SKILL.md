# Consumer Discretionary — Emerging Markets Growth Strategy

**Method:** Consumption Growth + Premiumization + Middle Class Expansion

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Regional Driver |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| **Revenue Growth** | 15-25% YoY | Nascent categories, market share |
| **Per-Capita Consumption Growth** | 5-10% | Rising incomes, middle class |
| **Premium Product Mix Growth** | 20%+ | Aspirational consumption (India: premium FMCG 15%+) |
| **Operating Margin** | Stable/expanding | Scale reaching critical point |
| **Market Share Gains** | Visible | Penetration of urban/rural split |

## India Example (2024-2025)

- Q3 2025 Consumer Discretionary surge +34.8% earnings
- Driven by: low rates, wage growth, consumer confidence
- Premium segments growing 20-25% vs core 8-10%
- Valuation risk: expanded on optimism; watch if growth disappoints
