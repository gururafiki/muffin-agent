# Consumer Staples — Emerging Markets Value Strategy

**Method:** P/E + Local Moat + Pricing Power Demonstration

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Regional Characteristic |
|--------|--------|-------------------------|
| **P/E Ratio** | 12-18x | Discount to DM 30-40% |
| **Revenue Growth** | 8-12% YoY | Higher than DM; category expansion |
| **Operating Margin** | 10-18% | Lower efficiency than DM peers (scale) |
| **Pricing Sustainability** | Demonstrated | Consumer acceptance of price increases |
| **Distribution Network** | Strengthening | Reach in rural markets |
| **Currency Risk** | Hedged or understood | Real rupee depreciation impacts |

## India Specific

- FMCG growth 8-10% YoY but driven by pricing (volume growth 3-4%)
- Premium segment growing 15%+ (aspirational consumption)
- Distribution cost higher (rural penetration expensive)
- Pricing power demonstrated: 2021-2024 multiple price increases accepted
