# Telecommunications — Developed Markets Growth Strategy

**Method:** ARPU Expansion + 5G Monetization + Enterprise Revenue Growth

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Driver |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **ARPU Growth** | 3-5% YoY | 5G premium pricing, bundling |
| **5G Subscriber Mix** | Rising toward 50%+ | Higher-margin services |
| **Enterprise Revenue Growth** | 8-12% YoY | B2B opportunities (edge computing, private networks) |
| **EV/EBITDA** | 7-9x | Premium for growth |
| **Capex Intensity** | 18-22% of revenue | Necessary for 5G deployment |

## 5G Monetization Potential

- Premium data packages: +$5-10/month per user
- Enterprise services: network slicing, edge computing, private networks (higher margins)
- IoT and M2M: nascent market, significant upside potential
